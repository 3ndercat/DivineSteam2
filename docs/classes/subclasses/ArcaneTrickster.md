## Level 3
### Spellcasting
You have learned to recognize the magic which lingers in the surrounding world, and to be able to channel it through your body. This channeling is not without strain. You have a pool of ***Appetance*** equal to your Rogue level, when casting spells this costs the level of the spell in ***Appetance***. All spent ***Appetance*** is restored when finishing a Long Rest.

#### Spell learning
You can cast and learn spells from **scrolls**. It also possible to learn from **passed on knowledge**, or rugirous **observation** within the world. You can learn difficulty 1 spells by heart, you can cast difficulty 2 spells but need to read from **scrolls** them while casting.
