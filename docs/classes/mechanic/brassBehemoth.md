# Brass behemoth
The brass behemoth is a true powerhouse, using equipment to extend their bodies power and prowess.

## Pneumatic gloves
Cost: 3 Materia
Body part: both lower arms
You have advantage on strength checks. +2 on str To-Hit rolls.

## Stabilizer
Cost: Materia
Body part: chest
You have advantage on ability checks made to resist being shoved or knocked prone.
