## Kinetic armour
Cost: 2 Materia <br>
Medium armour | AC: 13+Dex mod(max 2)

Kinetic armour is a double layered leather armour construction with springs in between. These springs absorb impacts(-2 damage if bludgeoning). When hit these springs latch into tension and will release on the next hit. When released the enemy will be brought off balance(If wielding the hitting weapon) and needs to roll a dex save.DC: 10+Dex mod
