# Nechrochanic
The nechrochanic is an individual who uses their knowledge of machinery and clockwork to reanimate the dead to do their bidding.

## Level 3
### Your body is not done ticking
During a short rest you can perform surgery on a fresh humanoid corpse to repurpose their body. Replacing the hart with a mechanical pump, embedding a device in the lower neck to influence the nervous system. Various hoses, ticking machines and pipes are running in and out of the body to keep it functioning.

What was once a person is now your minion, an unholy combination of flesh and machine. A clockwalker. You can have one clockwalker at a time.

The clockwalker uses the zombie statblock, but instead of undead it is a construct. It understands simple commands and is placed after the necrochanic in turnorder.
