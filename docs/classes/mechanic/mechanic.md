### Proficiencies:
Armor: Light armor, medium armor, shields<br>
Weapons: Simple weapons<br>
Tools: Tinker´s tools and Artisans tools of choice<br>
Saving Throws: Constitution, Intelligence<br>
Skills: Choose two from History, Investigation, Insight, Sleight of hand, Medicine, Perception and Sleight of hand

## Level 1
### Mechanics tinkering
The mechanic carries a certain amount of materials on themselves which they use to create their equipment and devices. This is a pool called: Materia. Every piece of equipment/device has a certain cost, the pool grows throughout the levels as they get more pro- and efficient. At level 1 there is 2 materia available.





## Level 3
### Mechanical philosophy
At third level you continue into a specific mechanical philosophy, these have specific equipments, devices and abilities.
