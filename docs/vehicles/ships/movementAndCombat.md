Sailships are what makes trade and transportation over seas possible. Driven by the wind and hardworking sailors, the journeys are intensive and not without peril over the onforgiving sea. Not to mention encountering enemy ships or pirates.

# Ship life
In this chapter ship life en ship movement in normal conditions(outside of combat) is described. 



# Ship to ship combat
Ship to ship combat happens on a hexagon map. Ships can range in length from 1-3 hexes. Their speed and nimblesness depends on the ship.

Movement during combat happens pre-actively. Movement for ships is decided one round in advance. Possible movements are ship dependent but usually include a certain amount of hexes forward and or a turn.
Certains movements are locked behind amount of sails deployed, the more sails deployed the faster a ship goes, but less nimble it becomes.

Players can take certains positions and actions aboard the ship. All actions can be fullfilled by the crew, but with a player also present in the position these actions have a higher chance of success.
